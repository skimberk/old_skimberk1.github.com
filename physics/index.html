Seatbelt Safety

In 2009 alone, 33,308 people were killed in car accidents, and another 2.3 million had to be hospitalized because of their injuries.

Wearing a seatbelt has been proven to be the single best way to prevent injuries (and death), and yet for some reason, millions of people drive without one every day.

What you can do

  • Wear a seat belt!
  • If you're a passenger in a car, make sure everyone is wearing their seatbelts.
  • If you're the driver, don't start driving until everyone's seatbelt is on (including your own).
